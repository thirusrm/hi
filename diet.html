3-Day Diet Chart

Follow this 3-day plan to help manage your blood sugar levels effectively.

Day 1

Meal Time Food
Breakfast 7:00 AM Oats with low-fat milk, 1 boiled egg, green tea
Mid-Morning Snack 10:30 AM 1 small apple or 6-8 almonds
Lunch 12:30 PM 2 whole-wheat rotis, mixed vegetables, grilled fish/tofu
Evening Snack 4:00 PM 1 cup roasted chickpeas or walnuts
Dinner 7:30 PM Vegetable soup, salad, grilled chicken/paneer

Day 2

Meal Time Food
Breakfast 7:00 AM Multigrain toast with peanut butter, 1 small orange
Mid-Morning Snack 10:30 AM 1 cup low-fat yogurt with chia seeds
Lunch 12:30 PM Brown rice, dal, steamed vegetables
Evening Snack 4:00 PM 1 small handful of nuts
Dinner 7:30 PM Grilled fish/tofu with mixed green salad

Day 3

Meal Time Food
Breakfast 7:00 AM Scrambled eggs with spinach, whole wheat toast
Mid-Morning Snack 10:30 AM 1 small pear
Lunch 12:30 PM Quinoa salad with chickpeas and veggies
Evening Snack 4:00 PM Roasted pumpkin seeds
Dinner 7:30 PM Lentil soup with sautéed vegetables

Instructions

1. Stick to a consistent meal schedule to stabilize blood sugar levels.
2. Avoid sugary beverages and processed foods.
3. Drink plenty of water throughout the day.

Healthy Tips

• Incorporate fiber-rich foods into your diet.
• Exercise regularly for at least 30 minutes a day.
• Monitor blood sugar levels as recommended by your doctor.

Healthcare Methods

Follow-up with your healthcare provider regularly and discuss your meal plan, medications, and exercise routines for optimal diabetes management.

Additional Information

For a customized diet plan, consult a certified nutritionist. Stay informed on the latest diabetes care recommendations and ensure balanced nutrition.
